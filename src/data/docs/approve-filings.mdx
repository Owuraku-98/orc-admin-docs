# Steps to Approve Filings

Locate the Filing
On the dashboard, go to the section “Amendments & Filings Pending Approval.”
Use the filter options at the top (Entity Type, Service Type, Payment Type, Date Range, or Search) to find the filing you want to review.

![2000X1238](/approve-filings.png)

## Open the Transaction Details
Click on the transaction number (e.g., 20251024000008987) under the Transaction column.
This opens a detailed view of the filing, showing submitted information and attached documents.

![2000X1238](/approve-filings1.png)

## Review Submission Details
Check all details under:
Service (e.g., Filings)
Entity Name (e.g., HISENSE)
Entity Type (e.g., Corporate Business Name)
Supporting documents and attachments.
Verify accuracy, completeness, and compliance with the requirements.

![2000X1238](/approve-filings2.png)

### Approve or Query

![2000X1238](/approve-filings3.png)
If all information is correct:
Click Approve (usually located at the bottom or top-right of the transaction page).
If something needs clarification:

![2000X1238](/approve-filings-approve.png)


Click Query, then enter your comments or requested changes.
The applicant will be notified to respond.

![2000X1238](/approve-filings-query.png)


####  Confirmation
Once approved, the transaction status changes from Pending to Approved.
If queried, the status changes to Queried, and the filing will move to the “Queried” section.

![2000X1238](/approve-filings4.png)
